About the Gooseman

I like to fly around and attack people for no good reason. No matter how fast you run, I guarantee you I am faster. My favorite hunting grounds are local parks and small lakes. I usually find that that lakes just don't make enough noise, so I enjoy squawking endlessly while you're trying to have a relaxing lakeside picnic. Even if you give me bread, I still won't shut up, becaue I hate you. I tend to make my nests in inconvenient places, like your front porch, and then get angry when you get too close. Sure, it used to be your house, but now it's mine. Another favorite pastime is crossing the road VERY slowly. I know I have wings, but I never feel like using them, so you'll just need to slam on your brakes and deal with it. Oh, I also poop on things. A lot of things. JUST BECAUSE I CAN.
